Negative effects of food production

Accounts for 70 % of the global water consumption

Agriculture occupies ½ of the habitable land

Livestock and feed prod. use 80 % of agricultural area

⅓ of global greenhouse gas emissions

Large amounts of waste

Global extensive deforestation

Wide use of pesticides

Emergence of antibiotic-resistant microorganisms

Ethical challenges with animal welfare

Problematic working conditions in the food industry

Sources: (NG, 2019) (FAO, 2020) (Ritchie, 2017)

cFood is inspired by UNs sustainable development goal #12 : Sustainable consumption and production. In order to establish sustainable food systems, the negative effects of food production must be reduced as much as possible. (NG, 2019)

How can I contribute?

The «right» choice?

Sustainable consumption and production is all about doing more with fewer resources. Consumers have a lot of power and our choices affect which products are produced and how.

It is of great importance that we are aware of the footprints behind the goods we buy, so that we can steer the food industry in a more sustainable direction. This is essential to ensure good living conditions for current and future generations.

Challenges for you as a consumer:

Many alternative items to choose from

Lack of time and energy to further examine each item

Limited access to simple, clear and comparable information

How are you going to make the «right» choices? This is where cFood comes in.
